Our Company

Alaylo Realty pvt. ltd. initially started in 2006 as an interior designing and execution firm. The transition into bungalow contracts was done in a short 3 year in the year 2009.

After completing many successful individual projects, ARPL was first registered with the MCA in 2015, with its entry into property development. Starting small and boost strapping its way through plot and bungalow developments, ARPL started its Vasai Beach Villas project in 2019 and found a responsive market for Beach cottages and resort communities.

Post successful selling of its 1st beach villas project, ARPL has now set sights on a larger development of resort community. Price consciousness and quality craftsmanship is the core of our organization.

Soaring Ahead


Alaylo Realty is currently developing a Resort community in the beautiful town of Vasai near Mumbai. The property is spread over 3 acres and has a beautiful beach connected to the property.

Alaylo Realty is also uniquely positioned to take advantage of a niche opportunity in the liasoning and land conversion market in India that offers high returns.

ARPL is targeting a turnover of 150 crores in fy 2021-22, while setting its sights firmly on 500 crores in fy 2022-23.

Our Concept

Second Home / Weekend Homes


Alaylo Realty has perfectly located properties in Bhuigaon in Vasai west for the purposes of weekend or second homes. These properties are clear title with road accessibility and 2-3 minutes walking distance of the beach.

With 80% of its 1st project sold out, the market need has been recognized by ARPL and is venturing into expanding its land bank.

Resort Communities


The latest project of ARPL is a concept of resort communities. The buyer gets to own a plot and cottage that is connected to a sprawling 1.5 acre resort.

You can enjoy your cottage in a well maintained and private environment while also enjoying the amenities of a cool resort.

When you are not using your cottage, you may rent it back to the resort and make a very substantial return of 24% on your investment.
